About CaniGesta

I built CaniGesta after a decade of working with veterinary clinics and breeding standard Labradors. I saw too many breeders struggle with manual calendars, confusing progesterone charts, and missing vital gestation scans. CaniGesta was designed to combine veterinary reproductive science with modern digital automation. It runs 100% locally in your browser, ensuring complete data privacy—no breeder details or mating dates are ever sold or stored. I hope this helps you raise healthy, happy litters!
