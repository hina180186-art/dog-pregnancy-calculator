About CaniGesta

CaniGesta was born from a personal experience — when my German Shepherd Bella had an unexpected early delivery at day 58, I had no tool that showed me what week she was in or what symptoms to expect. I scrambled through vet manuals and pregnancy forums at 2am, completely unprepared. I built CaniGesta so no breeder ever feels that way again.

Every milestone, every checklist item, every vet reminder in this tool comes from real canine reproductive science, reviewed by licensed theriogenologists. It's free because knowledge about your dog's pregnancy shouldn't cost anything.

— Mohid, Founder
