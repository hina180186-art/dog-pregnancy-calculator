Professional Veterinary Disclaimer

This Dog Pregnancy Calculator is designed for educational, breeder guidance, and scheduling estimations only. Canine gestation periods are subject to highly variable individual and breed deviations. It is critically important to partner with a qualified reproductive veterinarian to schedule clinical ultrasounds, perform pre-whelping skeletal X-rays (puppy count), and ensure full maternal maternal health. Never alter medication, diet, or whelping logistics without direct veterinary consultation.
