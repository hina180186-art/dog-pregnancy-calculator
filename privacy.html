Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Our Privacy Commitment

CaniGesta is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect no personal data. All pregnancy calculations, breed lookups, and timeline generation happen entirely in your browser (client-side). No data is sent to any server.

What We Do Not Collect

  • Dog names, mating dates, or any pregnancy data you enter
  • Email addresses (unless you voluntarily submit a reminder form)
  • Location data, IP addresses, or browsing history
  • Cookies for tracking or analytics

localStorage Usage

If you create an account or save litters, this data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time via your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We load Google Fonts (for typography) and the jsPDF library (for PDF generation) from external CDNs. These may log your IP address per their own privacy policies. We do not control or access these logs.

Contact

For any privacy questions, email us at privacy@canigesta.com or use the contact form.
