Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Who we are

CaniGesta is a privacy-first educational tool designed to help breeders track canine gestation timelines.

Data Collection

We do not collect or store any of your personal data on our servers. The entire CaniGesta application runs securely in your local browser.

LocalStorage Usage

When you use the "Save to My Litters" feature or log in, your dog's pregnancy profiles and session state are saved entirely in your device's localStorage. This means the data never leaves your device and is only accessible by you. We do not use cookies or tracking pixels.

No Third-Party Sharing

Because we do not possess your data, we cannot and do not share it with any third parties.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@canigesta.com.
